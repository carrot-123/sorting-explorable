Sorting with Computers

What is Sorting

If you've ever arranged your books by color, grouped poker cards by suits, or displayed your seashell collection by size, then you have sorted! Sorting simply refers to re-arranging elements in a certain order. Here's a quick example of why computers need to sort: Try finding and clicking on "aadvark" for both computers screens below. But for computers, sorting is not so trivial. Computers don't have eyes like we do, so they can't see the seashells all at once. Rather, they can only focus on one or two at a time. Let's now try sorting as though we are a computer.

How computers sort
